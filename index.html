Targeting & Data
Reaching the right professional audiences at scale
Data Strategy

Alkimi's targeting architecture operates in three layers — deployable individually or combined, with no reliance on cookies or persistent user IDs.

1 Placement Graph — Contextual Intelligence

Alkimi's proprietary content engine crawls and categorises publisher inventory in real time — analysing topic, keyword, editorial category, and semantic proximity. Campaigns target relevant content environments without any user-level data. Particularly effective for LinkedIn's B2B-adjacent audience: business news, finance, technology, leadership, and career content.

2 ALEx AI — Contextual Intelligence Layer

Built on the Placement Graph, ALEx AI adds behavioural inference and a performance feedback loop — identifying users whose content consumption signals affinity with the campaign, and progressively reallocating budget toward the highest-attention environments over the flight. Can be tuned to professional decision-maker and thought leadership content environments.

3 3rd Party Data — Audience Enrichment

50+ third-party data providers and DMPs available. Relevant for LinkedIn campaigns: Experian (senior professionals, high-income segments), Oracle (in-market B2B technology audiences), Eyeota (business decision-makers across EMEA), Nielsen (media consumption and household profile data).

Contextual-only option: Placement Graph + ALEx AI together provide a complete targeting solution with no audience data layer required — fully functional for cookieless environments and brands with strict data privacy requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions
Common questions from media teams evaluating Alkimi
FAQ

Why can't 100% Share of Screen be replicated through a standard DSP?

▾
Every other DSP views ad impressions in isolation — bidding on one slot at a time without awareness of other positions on the same page. Alkimi's technology operates at a screen level: analysing all available ad slots on a page simultaneously and bidding for them together. This is what makes a true 100% Share of Screen buy possible — owning every ad position at the moment the right user lands on the page. No other DSP can replicate this.

Does LinkedIn need bespoke creative for 100% SOS?

▾
No. 100% SOS uses standard IAB ad formats — the same creative assets LinkedIn would produce for any display campaign. No bespoke production, no publisher-by-publisher approval, and no additional lead time. If LinkedIn wants to elevate executions with video or interactive rich media, Alkimi Studio is available free of charge.

How does the Amazon DSP PMP work in practice?

▾
Alkimi curates a Private Marketplace deal with premium supply and targeting pre-applied. A Deal ID is provided directly into LinkedIn's Amazon DSP seat — LinkedIn's team then activates the deal, sets budgets, pacing, and frequency caps, and manages the campaign entirely within Amazon DSP. No IO required. Alkimi's preferred route for teams wanting direct control of the buy.

What markets can Alkimi support for LinkedIn campaigns?

▾
Alkimi operates across the UK, EMEA, MENA, KSA, APAC, the Nordics, and the US. Multi-market campaigns can be planned and activated as a single buy — no separate supplier relationships or local market negotiations required.

Are specific publishers guaranteed in a campaign?

▾
Publisher delivery on programmatic campaigns is real-time and auction-based. A fixed impression count on a named publication cannot be guaranteed. What Alkimi can guarantee: campaigns serve exclusively within a defined quality tier — no MFA sites, no unvetted inventory. If a guaranteed placement on a specific title is required for a line item, a PMP or direct deal alongside the programmatic campaign is the right approach.

How does CTV targeting work without cookies?

▾
Alkimi's CTV targeting operates at the household level using ACR (Automatic Content Recognition) data from smart TV panels, plus first-party streaming behaviour signals from AVOD and FAST platform partners. Parameters include content genre, viewing behaviour, device type, geographic location, and household profile. Combined addressable audience with full data layering: typically 8–10 million households across EMEA.

What is the lead time to get a campaign live?

▾
Managed service: Creative assets required at least 5 business days before launch. IO sign-off recommended with sufficient lead time for trafficking and QA.

Amazon DSP PMP (self-serve): Once a Deal ID is shared into LinkedIn's seat, activation timelines are controlled by LinkedIn's buying team. No IO required.

How does the on-chain model benefit LinkedIn as an advertiser?

▾
The practical outcome of Alkimi's on-chain infrastructure is lower network costs passed directly to advertisers as reduced fees — up to 70% lower than the industry average (ISBA benchmarked). More of every LinkedIn media budget reaches actual impressions and working media, rather than platform costs. This operates alongside standard third-party verification through IAS and DoubleVerify.
